Bemba Tribe

The Bemba are one of the largest ethnic groups in Zambia, mainly found in the Northern and Copperbelt Provinces. They are known for their strong chieftainship system and vibrant oral history.

Language and Culture

They speak **Chibemba** and celebrate ceremonies like *Ukusefya Pa Ng’wena*, which showcases their migration history. Proverbs, music, and praise poetry are central in Bemba tradition.
