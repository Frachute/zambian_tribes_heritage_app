Ngoni Tribe

The Ngoni are descendants of the Zulu warriors and settled in the Eastern Province of Zambia. They are famous for their military structure, traditional attire, and deep respect for ancestral values.

Their traditional ceremony, *Nc’wala*, is a grand celebration held in February, where the chief tastes the first fruits of the harvest and the people honor their Ngoni warrior roots with dance and dress.
